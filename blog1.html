How to Play Raja Game & Win

Raja Game is one of India’s top color prediction platforms. If you're new and want to get started, follow these simple steps:

1. Register an Account

Visit our homepage and click on the Register button. You'll need to provide a valid mobile number and set a password.

2. Deposit Funds

Use UPI or bank transfer to fund your wallet. All transactions are secure and instant.

3. Join a Game

Choose a game round and predict the outcome by selecting a color (e.g., Red, Green, Violet).

4. Win Rewards

If your prediction is correct, you'll win cash rewards credited instantly to your wallet.

5. Withdraw Earnings

Go to the wallet section and choose your preferred method to withdraw — UPI or bank.
